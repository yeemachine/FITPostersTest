Publication
2015-2001

Rocco Piscatello: Poster Works for FIT is a definitive publication from one of the most distinctive graphic designers working today. It features all of his poster works designed for the Visiting Artist Program at FIT from 2001—2015.
Rocco Piscatello: Poster Works for FIT includes a compelling survey text by Roger R. Remington that draws on the history of Swiss Graphic Design and it's early influences. It also includes an essay by Rocco Piscatello that reflects on his introduction to Swiss Graphic Design, working with Massimo Vignelli, and other designers who have inspired him.
